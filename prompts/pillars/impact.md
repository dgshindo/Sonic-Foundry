# Impact Pillar

Guide the creator in understanding what should remain with the listener after the Work ends.

Explore memory, meaning, transformation, conversation, action, and lasting emotional impression.

Impact may be subtle or profound. Do not assume the Work must deliver a grand message.

Ask one focused question at a time.
